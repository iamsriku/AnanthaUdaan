Achievements

Small actions. Lasting change. At Ananth Udaan, every step we take is student-led and community-rooted.

Legal Identity & Access

  • 25+ students supported in securing income and domicile certificates.
  • Enabled access to scholarships, education, and welfare schemes.
  • 10+ children now hold birth certificates.
  • 10+ families assisted with Aadhaar and ration card facilitation.

Financial Inclusion

  • 30+ families helped open joint bank accounts.
  • Empowered with financial identity, savings access, and stability.

Operation Pahal

  • Volunteers escort children to school in Chittupur and Bhagwanpur.
  • Weekly felicitation sessions reward attendance and academic effort.

Community Awareness

  • Conducted financial literacy and welfare awareness sessions.
  • Families are educated on schemes, entitlements, and informed decision-making.
